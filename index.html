About Me

Hi, I'm Anjalee!! I grew up in Northville, MI and our downtown is filled with Victorian-era architecture and small coffee shops. From a young age, my family and I have been traveling from Alaska all the way to South Africa (My scariest experience was when I was left behind in the Sydney subway station), so travel is a big passion of mine! At home, I have a puppy named Luna and I really enjoy working with/teaching kids about coding in my local elementary. My favorite activity is dancing whether it be ballet or other classical forms. In my free time I like to read/watch Sci-Fi novels and shows (The Handmaid's Tale and The 100 are my go tos). I'm hoping to pursue a career where I can use my passion for accessible design and marketing to improve consumers' lives within the technology industry.

As a junior at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree in Business and User Experience Design (UX) with interests in technology and marketing, I've learned a lot and I can't wait to learn more. My growth isn't confined to the classroom though, with my involvements as VP of Business Development in Community Consulting Club and as a teacher for Seven Mile Coding bridging the digital divide, I know that there are so many ways to empower and understand one another. This site is meant to be a creative outlet where I can show you my work and my passions. Please feel free to reach me at 734-890-8743 or panjalee@umich.edu!
